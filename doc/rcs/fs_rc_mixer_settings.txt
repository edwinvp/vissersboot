
CH1 = Left Motor
CH2 = Right Motor

Reversed channels: none

The first mix causes that...
- steering left would increase right motor.
- steering right would increase left motor

Mix #1:
Mix is: On
Master: CH1
Slave: CH2
Pos. mix: -100%
Neg. mix: -100%
Offset: 0%

The second mix causes that...
- steering up would increase not only the right motor but also the left motor.
- steering down would decrease not only the right motor but also the left motor.

Mix #2:
Mix is: On
Master: Ch2
Slave: Ch1
Pos. mix: 100%
Neg. mix: 100%
Offset: 0%
